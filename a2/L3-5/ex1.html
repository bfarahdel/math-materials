Example 1

Equation with Rational Roots

Page 191
Problem Statement
Solve \( x^2 + 6x + 9 = 36 \) by using the Square Root Property.
\( (x + 3)^2 = 36 \)
1 Factor the perfect square trinomial.
\( x + 3 = \pm\sqrt{36} \)
2 Apply the Square Root Property.
\( x + 3 = \pm 6 \)
3 Simplify the square root.
\( x = -3 \pm 6 \)
4 Subtract 3 from each side.
\( x = 3 \text{ or } x = -9 \)
5 Write as two equations and simplify.

Solution Set

\( \{-9, 3\} \)
