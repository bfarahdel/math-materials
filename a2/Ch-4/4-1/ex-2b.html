Term 1: The Root Problem 第一项：根号的问题

The first term is \(\sqrt{x}\). A polynomial term must have a non-negative integer exponent. \(\sqrt{x}\) is \(x^{1/2}\).

第一项是 \(\sqrt{x}\)。多项式的项必须具有非负整数指数。而 \(\sqrt{x}\) 等于 \(x^{1/2}\)。

Term 2: Standard Monomial 第二项：标准单项式

The second term is \(x\). This is a valid monomial with degree 1 (\(x^1\)).

第二项是 \(x\)。这是一个有效的单项式，次数为 1 (\(x^1\))。

Because the expression contains \(\sqrt{x}\), it fails the definition of a polynomial.
