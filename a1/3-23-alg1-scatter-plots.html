⚠️ THE INVASION / 入侵

The rapid increase in Zebra Mussel population is creating a massive issue. We need to analyze this population growth by performing data analysis.

斑马贻贝数量的快速增长正引发严重问题。我们需要通过数据分析来研究这一数量增长情况。

Data: Information, often numbers, collected for analysis.

数据： 通常为数字，收集用于分析的信息。

Bivariate Data: Data with two variables, such as year (x) and population (y).

双变量数据： 具有两个变量的数据，例如年份 (x) 和数量 (y)。

2. LINE OF BEST FIT / 最佳拟合线

Line of Best Fit: A straight line that best represents the data trend used for analysis and predictions. 最佳拟合线： 最能代表数据趋势的直线，用于分析和预测。

Step A: Find the slope (m):

Selected Points: P1(1, 15) and P2(7, 483).
Note: Any two points on the line can be picked to calculate m.
选定点：(1, 15) 和 (7, 483)。提示：可以选择直线上的任意两个点。

m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1) = (483 - 15) / (7 - 1) = 78

Step B: Find the y-intercept (b):

15 = 78(1) + b → b = -63

Equation: y = 78x - 63

3. PREDICTION / 预测

y = 78x - 63

Calculate the prediction for the year 2039 (where x = 30):

预测 2039 年的情况（其中 x = 30）：

y = 78(30) - 63

y = 2340 - 63 = 2277

Remember: Y is in thousands. 2277 × 1,000 =

2,277,000 Zebra Mussels

结论：2,277,000 只斑马贻贝。
